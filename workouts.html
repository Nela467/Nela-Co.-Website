At Home Workouts

Full Body Circuit

  • 10 Squats
  • 10 Push-ups
  • 20 Jumping Jacks
  • 10 Lunges (each leg)
  • 15-Second Plank

Repeat this circuit 3-5 times for a quick and effective home workout.

Core Strength

  • 20 Bicycle Crunches
  • 15 Leg Raises
  • 30-Second Plank
  • 20 Russian Twists

Perform each move slowly, focusing on your form. Complete 3 sets.

Yoga Flow

  • Downward Dog (30 seconds)
  • Warrior II (30 seconds each side)
  • Child's Pose (1 minute)
  • Cat-Cow (1 minute)

This gentle sequence will stretch and strengthen your muscles. Breathe deeply throughout.
