Breathing & Relaxation Techniques

Box Breathing: Inhale 4 sec → hold 4 sec → exhale 4 sec → hold 4 sec, repeat 5–10 rounds.
4-7-8 Technique: Inhale 4 sec → hold 7 sec → exhale 8 sec, repeat 4 rounds.
Body Scan: Lie down, close your eyes, mentally scan from head to toes, relaxing each part.
Mindful Pause: Stop for 1–2 minutes, focus on your breath and senses, letting thoughts drift by.
